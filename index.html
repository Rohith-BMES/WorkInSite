WorkInSite Software for Mobile Devices

WorkInSite Mobile is a mobile app for mobile phones that requires no installation on the device. It's ideal for use on the construction site or on the go with an active internet connection. The web server-based module can be used in any browser.

On the go and everywhere

  • Recording of timesheets, optionally with punch time.
  • Recording vacation requests.
  • View/download pay slips as a PDF.
  • Printing timesheets.
  • Review of recorded hours and approval.
  • Recording of equipment bookings and load across construction sites.
  • Display of basic construction site data.
  • Recording of construction site queries, complaints, defects.
  • Recording of daily construction reports/wage slips.
  • View of the service list with titles and items.
  • View the most important information on the service list.
  • Creation of shipping notices.
  • Retrieving device locations.
  • Insights into your own deployment planning.
  • Display of your own company logos and headers.

Quick answers to common questions about WorkInSite

Creating your first site is simple:

1) Open the WorkInSite app.
2) Tap the yellow "New Site" button on your dashboard.
3) Fill in the site details including name, location, and description.
4) Set up your team members and their roles.
5) Configure attendance settings.
6) Save your site.
